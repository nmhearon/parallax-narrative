A Day in the Life of a Northwest Kid

The northwest is a place that values the whole "work hard play hard" mantra. Of course you've gotta make a living, but you can't let it stop you from doing what you love. The northwest is home to thousands of phenomenal hiking trails and an excellent place for the outdoor enthusiast.

Not to mention, when you're hiking, you get excellent views of Mt. Rainier.

And after you're done with a hike, you can go take a dip in the puget sound.

At the end of the day, you can go home to one of the most beautiful cities in the country and enjoy a good craft beer.
